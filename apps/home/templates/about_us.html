About Us

Welcome to AK Auto Detail, your trusted auto detailing specialists.

We are a trusted small business in the Sacramento region, providing premium detailing services for public and private dealership vehicles.

Customer satisfaction is our priority. Whether you need a quick wash or full detailing, we guarantee exceptional results and a showroom finish.

Thank you for choosing AK Auto Detail. We look forward to keeping your car looking brand new!
